---

### Example: Fatigue Detection

**Input:**
- Time-series performance data: Daily spend, CTR, and CVR
- Creative Metadata: Campaign start date and historical benchmarks

**Pipeline:**
A model or anomaly detection identifying when performance drops below a standard deviation relative to launch.

**Output:**
- Actionable Insight: A "Creative Health" score (0–100%)
- Recommendation: "Pause ad" or "Pivot creative" suggestion

---

### Example: Explainability

**Input:**
- Raw Image/Video Assets
- Performance Metrics: High-performing vs. low-performing labels based on KPI data

**Pipeline:**
Use a Computer Vision model to extract features such as "Call to Action" (CTA) button color or text density. Feed these visual features into an LLM to generate a natural language explanation.

**Output:**
- Insight: *"Ads with 'Play Now' buttons in neon green perform 15% better in this category."*
- AI Copilot Chat: A conversational answer explaining why a specific asset is successful
